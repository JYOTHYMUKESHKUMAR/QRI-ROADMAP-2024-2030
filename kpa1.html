Overview

This key priority area focuses on ensuring that all individuals in Qatar have equitable access to high-quality rehabilitation services. It emphasizes delivering care that is efficient, patient-centered, accessible, and integrated across the continuum of care.

Overall Progress

30% Complete

SO 1: High-Value, Efficient Care Delivery

Implementing cost-effective treatment models and optimizing resource utilization to deliver high-value, efficient rehabilitation care.

  • Implementation of Cost-Effective Treatment Models
  • Integrate digital health tools
  • Resource Management
  • Performance Metrics and Outcome Measurement
  • Financial Sustainability Programs
  • Patient Flow Optimization
  • Staff Training and Lean Management
  • Collaboration and Partnerships
  • Eco-Friendly Practices
  • Patient Education and Self-Management

SO 2: Trusted Patient-Centered Rehabilitation Care

Enhancing patient engagement and personalized care to build trust and improve patient experience throughout the rehabilitation journey.

  • Enhanced Patient Engagement Platforms
  • Patient and family advocacy Programs
  • Personalized Rehabilitation Plans
  • Improved Patient, family, and caregiver Education
  • Quality of Life Assessments
  • Integration of Holistic Health Approaches
  • Patient Experience Officers

SO 3: Accessible and Inclusive Rehabilitation Care

Creating age and mobility-friendly environments and ensuring universal access to rehabilitation services for all populations.

  • Age and Mobility-Friendly hospitals
  • Hospital wide 'Activity and Mobility Program' (AMP)
  • Universal Access to Assistive Technology
  • Access Enhancement Through Digital Platforms
  • Equity in Service Provision
  • Integration of Services
  • New rehabilitation Programs

SO 4: Integrated Care Across the Continuum

Ensuring seamless transitions and coordination across different levels of care to provide comprehensive rehabilitation services.

  • Continuum of Care Framework Development
  • Interdisciplinary culture development & team Coordination
  • Patient Navigation and Case Management
  • Technology Integration for Seamless Care Transitions
  • Early and Continuous Rehabilitation Assessment
  • Feedback Mechanisms for Patient and Caregiver
  • Sustainable Funding Models
